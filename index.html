About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, also known as Los Blancos, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. The club is one of three founding members of La Liga that have never been relegated from the top division since its inception in 1929, along with Athletic Bilbao and Barcelona2. Real Madrid is one of the world’s best-known teams, with fans in many countries1 . The club holds many long-standing rivalries, most notably El Clásico with Barcelona and El Derbi Madrileño with Atlético Madrid.
